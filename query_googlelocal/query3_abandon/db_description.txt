You are working with two databases to solve the query: "What are the top 5 most talked-about business categories based on review volume, and what is the average rating for each?"

Here are the description of these two databases:
1. review_database
- This database is in SQLite database format and contains review information from Google Maps (reviewer name, ratings, text, etc.) up to Sep 2021 in the United States.
- Fields:
    - name (str)
    - time (str)
    - rating (int)
    - text (str)
    - gmap_id (str) 

2. business_database
- This database is stored as MySQL format and contains business metadata from Google Maps (business name, description, hours, etc.) up to Sep 2021 in the United States.
- Fields:
    - name (str)
    - gmap_id (str)
    - description (str)
    - num_of_reviews (int)
    - hours (list)
    - MISC (dict)
    - state (str)
